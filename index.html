Yarn Dyeing Excellence

For more than two decades we've been refining the craft of yarn dyeing; combining careful attention to detail with a deep understanding of colour. It's what we do, and what we take pride in.

The Art of Dyeing reflects how we work; with consistency, care and a commitment to doing things properly.

About Us

Based in the heart of West Yorkshire's historic textile industry, Calder Dyeing is a specialist yarn dye house with strong technical expertise and a practical, hands-on ethos.

We work closely with our customers to deliver consistent, high-quality dyeing, across a wide range of yarns and processes - from fibre and hank to package dyeing. With a focus on reliability, efficiency and long-term partnerships, we support brands and manufacturers both in the UK and internationally.
